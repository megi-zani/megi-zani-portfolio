About

A design background rooted in marketing, education and visual storytelling.

I am a multidisciplinary designer with experience across branding, social media, and web design. I enjoy translating a brand's values into visual systems – from logos and colour palettes to layouts for campaigns, catalogues and digital experiences.

Over the last years I have worked with travel agencies, energy companies and professional services, helping them define a consistent, premium visual language across print and digital. I combine a creative eye with solid organisational skills, used to managing projects end‑to‑end from concept to final assets.

Based in: Calabria, Italy
Working with: brands in tourism, energy, education and marketing
Languages: Albanian (native), Italian (C2), English (C2)
Focus: clean layouts, strong typography, and clear visual hierarchy
